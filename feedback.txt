Building hvault was a great experience, especially in how I was able to combine AWS KMS with Hedera. Integrating AWS KMS was straightforward—its Envelope Encryption model allowed me to secure patient data with hardware-level protection while keeping the user experience seamless. I really appreciated how AWS handles key isolation, which made it easy to provision unique master keys for every new user automatically.

Using Hedera for the audit trail and tamper detection was equally rewarding. The Hedera Consensus Service (HCS) is incredibly fast and cost-effective for recording file fingerprints. Seeing a real-time, immutable proof of every document access on HashScan gave me a lot of confidence in the integrity of the system. The combination of AWS's encryption and Hedera's public verifiability allowed me to build a truly "trustless" medical locker where patients don't have to just take my word that their data is safe—they can see the proof on the blockchain.
